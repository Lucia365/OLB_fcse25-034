WELCOME TO KWALAPE SAFARI LODGE

Kwalape Safari Lodge was established in 1999 in Botswana and has grown into one of the most welcoming safari destinations near Chobe National Park.

Our lodge provides affordable accommodation, relaxing facilities and exciting safari experiences for both local and international tourists.

Whether you are looking for peaceful relaxation, wildlife adventures or cultural experiences, Kwalape Safari Lodge offers comfort and unforgettable memories.

OUR VISION

To become Botswana’s leading safari lodge known for comfort, hospitality and unforgettable wildlife experiences.

OUR MISSION

To provide quality accommodation, excellent customer service and affordable safari experiences while promoting tourism and environmental conservation.
